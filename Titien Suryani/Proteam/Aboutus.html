OUR COMPANY
PT. Inkor Bola Pasific was established in 1992. It is now the biggest ball manufacturer in Indonesia with annual production capacity of 1,800,000 ball. We export 75% of our products to various countries in North and South America, Europe, Middle-East, Asia and Australia. Aside from producing our own Proteam brand we also manufacture balls for Tachikara brand and also accept orders with your design and specification
OUR PEOPLE
Our work force consists of 61 office staff, 500 employees, 400 stitch craftmen (no child labour)
